Speedruns are completing a game as fast as possible under certain restrictions such as not being able to see. The main website for speedruns is Speedrun.com

Scandals in the Speedrunning Community

Twin Galaxies has had a lot of talk about it. There was a speedrunner for the Atari game Dragster who was recently stripped of his records and got his world record taken away from him. He also had a Guinness world record taken away from him.
However, there is another, more infamous, more devious speedrunner. His name is Billy Mitchell. He set a bunch of records (he was the first person to apparently score over one million points) and actually was in a Donkey Kong documentary called King of Kong. However, there has been a bunch of evidence that he was cheating and the referee was actually the same guy who cheated at Dragster. Here's a video breaking down how exactly he cheated.
